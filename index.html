What we do

We have been teaching around 250 children of the age 5 to 15 in our classroom. We provide them books, copies, pens regularly. Some of them go to a nearby govt. School so we keep a check on their school work and their exams. We tell them about various competitive exams and scholarship tests.

Working for the:

children who don't go to school

drug/alcohol abusers

the homeless

people with learning and physical disabilities

How it all began

An idea struck my mind while I was traveling from Patna to Pathankot. It was a long journey and I didn't have an android phone. So, i was just looking through the glass window. Stations changed one by one. However, every station had a common scenario- The begging children.

Some were younger and some were of my age which was 15 that time. I was affected, tears rolled down my cheeks. While whimpering quietly, the only thought on my mind was if there was any way I could help them out. Soon after, I returned from the trip and I discussed this with my best friend Yashika. She motivated me to help the children around us. A few more friends joined me, we collected money and bought basic study kits for around 50 children. My father told me that education is the key to everything, it gives access to whatever we want in our life.

Since then, we have been teaching and sharing some beautiful moments with the less privileged children.

- Vaishnavi (Founder & Trustee)
